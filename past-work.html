Career Highlights

A Timeline of Milestones

  1. 2024

    "Vinyl Nights" charts on jazz radio

    The lead single from Vinyl Nights spent six weeks on regional jazz radio charts, earning airplay across three states.

  2. 2026

    Nashville Soul Award — Vocalist of the Year

    Recognized by the local arts council for outstanding contribution to the city's soul and jazz scene.

  3. 2021

    Debut singles "Free My Mind" and "She Ain't Me" released

    Independently produced and released, the album drew praise from local press for its confidence and restraint.

  4. 2025

    First headline show, The Amber Room

    A sold-out debut headline performance that launched a lasting relationship with one of the city's most beloved venues.
