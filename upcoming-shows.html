Seating & Access

Most venues offer intimate table seating with limited capacity — arrive early for the best sightlines. Accessible seating is available on request; contact the venue directly using the details on your ticket confirmation.

Ages & Etiquette

Lounge and supper club shows typically run 21+; festival dates are all-ages. These are listening rooms — we ask for hushed tones during ballads and warm applause for the band.
